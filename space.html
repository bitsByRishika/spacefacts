Planets Of Our Solar System:

  1. MERCURY-The smallest and closest to sun.
  2. VENUS-The hottest planet due to its thick atmosphere.
  3. EARTH-The only known planet with life.
  4. MARS-Known as the Red Planet; has the tallest volcano in the solar system (Olympus Mons).
  5. JUPITER-The largest planet, famous for its Great Red Spot.
  6. SATURN-Has the most spectacular ring system.
  7. URANUS-Has the most spectacular ring system.
  8. NEPTUNE-The farthest planet, known for its strong winds and deep blue color.
